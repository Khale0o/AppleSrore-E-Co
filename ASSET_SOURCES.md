# Asset Sources

All active product imagery is original, unbranded concept artwork generated locally for this educational portfolio project on 2026-07-19. No Apple logo, product-page photography, packaging, watermark, or third-party commercial artwork is included. Chroma-key generation outputs were converted to transparent PNGs and stored under `assets/products/`.

## Active generated renders

- iPhone: 8 files covering six models plus Deep Blue and Graphite Black finish variants for iPhone 17 Pro.
- Mac: 7 files covering every visible Mac model.
- iPad: 5 files covering every visible iPad model.
- Watch: 3 files covering every visible Watch model.
- AirPods: 3 files covering every visible AirPods model.
- Accessories: 4 files covering every visible accessory model.

The 30 active files are mapped directly from the 28 catalog product IDs. Every exposed finish maps to its own existing local file; unavailable finishes remain hidden. The original six family-level concept renders remain in the asset directories for historical comparison but are not used by normal product paths.

Generated renders are concept illustrations, not official product photography. Product names are used descriptively in an unofficial app that is not affiliated with or endorsed by Apple Inc.
