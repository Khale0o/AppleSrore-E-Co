# Asset Sources

Six original generated neutral product-family assets are included locally: iPhone, Mac, iPad, Watch, AirPods, and Accessories under `assets/products/`. They are unbranded original concept imagery generated for this project, not Apple product imagery. All 28 catalog entries resolve to one of these local family assets.

Before use, record each selected official Apple Newsroom or product-page source URL, the applicable permission/usage basis, downloaded filename, and optimization details here. Apple-owned imagery must not be added to this unofficial portfolio storefront without a documented rights basis. These original family assets may be manually replaced later with rights-cleared product imagery.
