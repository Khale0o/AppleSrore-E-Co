# Design Direction

## Brand position

AppleStore is a premium cinematic technology-commerce concept. It is unofficial and unaffiliated with Apple Inc.; it must not use the Apple logo or impersonate an official store. The experience should feel like an interactive product film, not a standard e-commerce template.

## Visual language

- Primarily dark scenes using black, graphite, silver, soft white, and subtle cool-blue highlights; gold is never a primary accent.
- Use large typography, generous negative space, product imagery as the main focus, and minimal visible chrome.
- Use soft gradients and controlled lighting. Glass effects are permitted only when functionally justified.

## Avoid

- Generic product grids, repeated card layouts, template-like commerce patterns, crowded controls, and bright multi-color palettes.
- Heavy glassmorphism, excessive blur, decorative motion without purpose, and fake luxury achieved solely with gold accents.

## Composition rules

- Give each scene one dominant focal product; text and controls support rather than compete with it.
- Keep primary actions easy to reach, hierarchy clear on small screens, and critical information away from screen edges.
- Reserve clear space for Hero transitions and product motion.

## Ecosystem direction

The complete ecosystem can include smartphones, laptops, tablets, smart watches, earbuds, and accessories. The first vertical slice uses one main smartphone and one secondary smartphone; supporting ecosystem imagery is atmosphere only where needed. Do not design the full ecosystem flow yet.
